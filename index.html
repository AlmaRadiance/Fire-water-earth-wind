Game Rules

Welcome to the Elemental Adventure Game! In this game, you will face off against the computer in a battle of elements: Fire, Water, Earth, and Wind.

Here are the rules:

  • Each player selects an element.
  • The computer will also randomly choose an element.
  • The winner is determined based on the elemental strengths and weaknesses.
  • The game consists of 5 rounds.
  • May the best element prevail!
