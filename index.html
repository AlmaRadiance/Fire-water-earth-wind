Welcome to the Elemental Adventure Game, where ancient powers clash!

Rules

In this mystical world, you possess the power of Fire, Water, Wind, and Earth. Brace yourself for epic battles against the computer!

  • Each player selects an element.
  • The computer will also randomly choose an element.
  • The winner is determined based on the elemental strengths and weaknesses.
  • Prepare for an epic contest spanning five rounds. Every selection, every move, will determine the course of the battle.
  • With every round, the stage is set for the ultimate victor. Choose your element wisely, for in this elemental saga, only the mightiest will triumph!

The Elements

  • Fire: Fierce and unyielding, Fire burns Wind but is extinguished by Water.
  • Water: Fluid and adaptable, Water douses Fire but is swept away by Wind.
  • Wind: Swift and powerful, Wind extinguishes Fire but is resisted by Earth.
  • Earth: Steadfast and unyielding, Earth resists Wind but is eroded by Water.
